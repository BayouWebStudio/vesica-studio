Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Vesica Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates vesica.studio and the Vesica Studio application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you use Vesica Studio, we may collect:

  • Account information — Email address and name if you create an account or sign up for a subscription.
  • Usage data — Anonymized information about how you use the app, including features used, drawings created, and session data.
  • Device information — Basic device identifiers and browser type for app stability and analytics.
  • Content you create — Drawings and geometry you create within the app are stored securely and accessible only to you.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Vesica Studio service
  • To manage your account and process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To improve and optimize the app experience
  • To communicate with you about your account or service updates

Payments

Subscription payments are processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card or banking information on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy governs how your payment data is handled.

Data Storage & Security

Your drawings and account data are stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your information. Your creations remain yours — we do not claim ownership of any content you create.

Analytics

We may use anonymized analytics to understand how users interact with Vesica Studio. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Stripe — For payment processing
  • Web hosting providers — For app delivery
  • Email service providers — For account communication

Cookies

Vesica Studio may use cookies to maintain session state and for basic analytics. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Children's Privacy

Vesica Studio is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@vesica.studio.

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

Questions or concerns? Email support@vesica.studio.
