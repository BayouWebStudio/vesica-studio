Questions

Common questions.

Do I need to create an account?

Not for the Free plan. Open the app and start drawing immediately. An account is required for Pro and Lifetime to sync your gallery across devices.

Can I use Vesica Studio on my iPad?

Yes. Vesica Studio is a Progressive Web App — install it from Safari on your iPad and it works like a native app. Apple Pencil is fully supported with pressure sensitivity.

What does "Lifetime" actually mean?

One payment. You get Pro features forever, including all future Pro updates. No subscription, no renewal, no surprise charges.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?

Yes. Cancel any time — your access continues until the end of the billing period. No lock-in.

Is the Free plan actually useful?

Yes. All three symmetry modes, PNG export, and the full drawing toolset are free. Pro unlocks layers, blend modes, post-processing, and the gallery.
