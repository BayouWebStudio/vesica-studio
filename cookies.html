Cookies Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are and how Vesica Studio uses them on vesica.studio.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential cookies — Required for Vesica Studio to function properly, including session management and authentication for your account.
  • Functional cookies — Remember your preferences and settings within the app.
  • Analytics cookies — Help us understand how visitors interact with vesica.studio by collecting anonymized usage data.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services:

  • Stripe — For secure payment processing during subscription checkout.
  • Google Analytics — For website traffic analysis. You can opt out using browser extensions.

Managing Your Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. You can:

  • Use private/incognito mode
  • Block or delete cookies via browser settings
  • Install browser extensions that block tracking cookies

Note: Disabling essential cookies may prevent Vesica Studio from functioning properly.

More Information

For more information about cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.
