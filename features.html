Features

Every tool you need.

A professional symmetry drawing studio, free to start.

01 — Symmetry

Mandala Mode

Set any number of rotational segments from 2 to 128. Every stroke you make is instantly replicated around the center — in real time, with zero lag. Enable bilateral mirror for reflection symmetry on top of rotation.

  • 2 to 128 rotational segments
  • Bilateral mirror toggle per layer
  • Movable center point — off-center mandalas
  • Real-time replication as you draw

02 — Pattern

17 Wallpaper Groups

Mathematics proved there are exactly 17 ways to tile the plane with repeating symmetry. Vesica Studio implements all of them — the same crystallographic groups found in Islamic geometric art, Escher's tessellations, and ancient mosaic floors.

  • Complete 2D crystallographic classification
  • p1, p2, pm, pg, cm, pmm, pmg, pgg, cmm
  • p4, p4m, p4g, p3, p3m1, p31m, p6, p6m
  • Live tiling preview as you draw

03 — Kaleidoscope

Kaleidoscope Mode

Combine local rosette symmetry with global wallpaper tiling. Draw once — your stroke is reflected and rotated locally, then that entire cell is tiled across the plane. Patterns within patterns, infinitely.

  • Local rosette symmetry (rotational + mirror)
  • Global wallpaper group tiling
  • Adjustable rosette center per tile
  • Infinite plane preview with zoom

04 — Layers

Layer System

Work the way you think — build complexity gradually, non-destructively. Every layer can carry its own symmetry mode, completely independent of the rest. Blend modes let you mix geometry with photographic precision.

  • Unlimited layers
  • 16 blend modes (Multiply, Screen, Overlay, and more)
  • Per-layer symmetry mode overrides
  • Layer opacity and lock controls

05 — Tools

Drawing Tools

Every tool built for the way geometric artists actually work. The Dotwork brush simulates tattoo stippling with pressure-sensitive dot density — draw directly in the medium you'll tattoo in.

  • Brush — smooth pressure-sensitive strokes
  • Dotwork — stipple density maps to pressure
  • Eraser with symmetry awareness
  • Shapes and geometric primitives
  • Fill and Gradient Thickness

06 — Transforms

Post-Processing Transforms

Apply mathematical transformations to finished geometry. The Hyperbolic transform maps your artwork onto a Poincaré disk — the same non-Euclidean space Escher used for his Circle Limit series.

  • Hyperbolic (Poincaré disk projection)
  • Kaleidoscope transform
  • Repeat — tile your artwork
  • Polar — wrap flat patterns into circles

07 — Export

Export & Gallery

Export your work as high-resolution PNG — ready to send to clients, print as reference, or use in your portfolio. The favorites gallery keeps everything organized with tagging and full-text search.

  • PNG export at canvas resolution
  • Favorites gallery — save and revisit work
  • Tagging system for organization
  • Full-text search across your library
